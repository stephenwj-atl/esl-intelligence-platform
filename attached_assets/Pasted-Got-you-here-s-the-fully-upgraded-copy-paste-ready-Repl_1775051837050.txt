5. CAPITAL MODE LOGIC (VERY IMPORTANT)

5.1 LOAN MODE

Requires:
	•	lower risk tolerance
	•	higher confidence
	•	strong predictability

Rules:
	•	high risk → decline
	•	low confidence → pricing penalty
	•	requires clear mitigation path

⸻

5.2 GRANT MODE

Allows:
	•	higher risk
	•	lower confidence
	•	early-stage environments

BUT requires:
	•	high monitoring intensity
	•	structured milestones
	•	validation checkpoints

⸻

5.3 BLENDED MODE

Used when:
	•	risk moderate
	•	confidence moderate
	•	viability uncertain

Structure:
	•	grant absorbs early risk
	•	loan activates after validation
