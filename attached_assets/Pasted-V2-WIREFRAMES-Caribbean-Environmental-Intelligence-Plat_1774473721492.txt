2. RISK VS CONFIDENCE SCATTER (MOST IMPORTANT VISUAL)
Why this matters

This is where your intelligence becomes obvious instantly.

Design
Y-Axis: Confidence (0–100)
X-Axis: Risk (0–100)

Quadrants:

Top Left     → LOW RISK / HIGH CONFIDENCE (Ideal)
Top Right    → HIGH RISK / HIGH CONFIDENCE (Known Risk)
Bottom Left  → LOW RISK / LOW CONFIDENCE (Uncertain)
Bottom Right → HIGH RISK / LOW CONFIDENCE (Danger Zone)
Visual Behavior
Each dot = a project
Size of dot = investment amount
Color:
Green → low risk
Yellow → medium
Red → high
Insight Example (auto box beside chart):

3 projects fall in high-risk / low-confidence quadrant — representing $28M exposure requiring immediate validation
